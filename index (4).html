Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your construction or renovation project with Z&R.

What is the construction cost per marla in Islamabad in 2025? +
Construction costs vary based on finish level. Grey structure only: PKR 3,500–4,500 per sqft. Semi-furnished: PKR 5,000–7,000 per sqft. Fully furnished luxury: PKR 8,000–12,000+ per sqft. Z&R provides a completely free, fully itemized quote tailored to your exact project — with zero hidden costs.
How long does it take to build a house in Islamabad? +
A standard 5-marla home takes approximately 6–9 months from foundation to full finishing. A 10-marla home typically takes 10–14 months. We provide a detailed milestone timeline before any work commences and maintain a 98% on-schedule delivery record across all project types.
Do you handle CDA and RDA approvals and building permits? +
Yes, absolutely. Our team handles all CDA (Capital Development Authority) and RDA (Rawalpindi Development Authority) map submissions, approvals and NOCs. We have extensive experience navigating the approval process efficiently — saving you significant time, money and stress.
Can Z&R manage my project if I am living overseas? +
Absolutely — a large portion of our clients are overseas Pakistanis in the UK, USA, Canada and Gulf. We provide weekly WhatsApp and email progress reports with photos and videos. All documentation is handled digitally and payments can be made via bank transfer. We keep you fully in control at all times.
What warranty do you provide after project completion? +
Z&R provides a full 1-year structural warranty on all completed projects. Any structural defects appearing within 12 months of handover are fixed at zero cost to you. We also provide a 6-month warranty on plumbing and electrical works — all backed by a written, signed warranty agreement.
Do you work in Bahria Town, DHA and other housing societies? +
Yes, we work extensively in Bahria Town Rawalpindi, DHA Islamabad, Naval Anchorage, Faisal Town, Gulberg Residencia, Jinnah Garden and all F and G sectors of Islamabad. We are thoroughly familiar with each society's bylaws, NOC requirements and construction by-laws.
